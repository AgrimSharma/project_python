Upcoming Certification and Training
 

Did you know that Kanban empowers your team to operate at a high level of productivity because of its evolutionary approach to manage change in the organization? It accelerates maturity through high visualization, control of the amount of work that’s being done, and evolves your business to being “fit for purpose.” Sound interesting? Then the Kanban Foundation classes (KMP 1 & 2) are for you. We will explore simple concepts that will help you and your team organize, understand and manage work. KMP 1 & 2 will help you understand how Kanban fits within the agile construct and will provide you discrete techniques that you can use right away.

 

KMP 1 - South Portland, August 26 & 27 - 2016

This 2-day Foundations-level Kanban training class, certified by Lean Kanban University, is for managers, developers, and anyone wanting to learn the fundamentals of the Kanban Method. The class includes the use of a Kanban simulation as well as exercises to design an actual Kanban system.

KMP 2 - South Portland, September 9 & 10 - 2016

The KMP II: Kanban Management Professional class focuses on the rollout and daily operation of a Kanban system. You will consider what type of Kanban initiative is most appropriate for your organization and how to manage and evolve it over time without trauma.
Learn how to improve agility with asymmetrical commitment and cadences. The class also covers recommended meetings, handling metrics, and setting policy.
