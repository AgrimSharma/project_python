Affiliate Program – FAQs

General

What is an Affiliate?

An Affiliate is a person or company who links their website to ours and earns a commission on any resulting sales.

Who can be an Affiliate?

Anyone with a website can become an Affiliate. You can be from any country in the world and have any type of website, although we do retain the right to refuse or revoke membership to an Affiliate with a website containing objectionable material.

Why should I become an Affiliate?

Because it's free to become an Affiliate and you'll earn money easily. You'll also add value to your website by offering our products to your customers.

How do I become an Affiliate?

You can become an Affiliate by joining our Affiliate Program. Simply sign up.

How much does it cost?

It's totally free to join our Affiliate Program.

How does it work and how much can I earn?

For 12 months, half of all revenue generated from those users joining ScrumDo through your link is credited to you.

Links

How do I link to your website?

Sign up as a ScrumDo user and get the affiliate link to share it with others.

Where should I place the link?

The most effective place to place the link is at the top of your webpage or send as an email to friends/companies.

Who can I share the link with?

Anyone who can profit using Agile Project Management.

Payments

How do I track my status on payments?

Sign in to your ScrumDo account and Check the status of your affiliate Program earnings.

How do I get paid?

You should have a PayPal account, we will send the payment to your PayPal account every first week of the month.
